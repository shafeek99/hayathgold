About us

We are one of the oldest business families in India with a family legacy of 112 years in business. Started for the noble cause of nation-building and self-sustenance in a pre-independent India, the forefathers believed ethical, honest and transparent business practices should form the foundation of the group. Today, when I look around me, business is all about toplines, bottomlines and market capitalisation. But Hayath entered into business in a pre-independent India to build a better India, as our forefathers believed ethical, fair business will make India self-sufficient and strong.

Exclusive Brands
